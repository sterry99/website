About Me

Just a few interesting tidbits.

How I answer the question, "What makes you unique?"

I spell "dog", D-A-W-G. I'm the son of a prison warden (How you like that Gavin DeGraw!). I've tried for the past six years to get Shaq to tell my best friend "Happy Birthday" on Twitter.

Seriously though, I'm the husband to Mollie Kate and the father to Margaret. We live in Athens, Ga., home to the University of Georgia and the Georgia Theatre. When I'm not creating things for the internet, you can find me watching "The Office", taking far to long of a walk with my family, or chatting about how this year is the year the Dawgs will win it all!
